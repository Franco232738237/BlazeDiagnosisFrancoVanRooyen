Pod4(Next-feature)

 1. Quote Resubmission with Revision Tracking:
- Allow users to resubmit quotes when changes are required.
- Each resubmission should create a new revision number.
- Maintain a history log showing what was changed, by whom, and when.
- Ensure previous versions remain viewable for audit and compliance.

 2. Email Quote to Customer:
- Add functionality to send quotes directly via email from the system.
- Support customizable email templates.
- Track delivery status and customer receipt confirmation.

3. Quote Expiry Notifications:
- Automatically notify customers before a quote expires.
- Internal alerts for sales staff when quotes are nearing expiry.
- Configurable expiry periods (default 30 days, adjustable per quote).
- Option to extend expiry date with manager approval.
