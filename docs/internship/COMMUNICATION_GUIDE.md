# Communication Guide

## When to Communicate

Communicate early when:

- You are stuck.
- You are unavailable.
- You are writing exams.
- You do not understand a task.
- You need access to a tool or repository.
- You completed a task and need review.
- You found a bug or security concern.

## Good Technical Question Format

```text
I am trying to:
I expected:
What happened:
What I already tried:
File or task involved:
Screenshot or error message:
```

## Availability Format

```text
I am available on:
I am not available on:
I have exams or study commitments on:
Best time for a short check-in:
```

## Progress Update Format

```text
Today I worked on:
I completed:
I learned:
I am blocked by:
Next I will:
```
